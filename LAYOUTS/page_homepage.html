Digital Commons project is the project of the Liverpool City Region Civic Data Cooperative team. We are focused on collecting datasets from Liverpool City Region that are mainly related to healthcare.

Since we have started with the Digital Commons project, we have collected ample helpful data. Our main mission is to share collected datasets with businesses and people across the region.
